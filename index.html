We conduct various events

We conduct various events that hone an individual's overall personality and interpersonal skills. Also, soft skills are very important in today's world, so one ought to work towards improving it.

Abhyudaya

Abhyudaya, signifies the sunrise, is the annual fest of Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Gorakhpur. It is a conglomeration of cultural, literary and fine arts events which will serve to create a marvellous festive gala worth relishing and partaking. The fest furnishes you with a procession of multifarious events that would surely have an awe-strucking impact. The majority of the events are in-venue, there are some online events viz. Scribbles, Photoholic, Rising Star. In-venue events include Literati (poetry and articles), Don de Paperica, Rangoli, Face Painting, Hair Styling, Hermosa (thematic fashion show), Dancing, Singing, Dramatics and umpteen more!

Tiresia

We publish a monthly newsletter, Tiresia in which we include interview of prominent persons under a section called tete-a-tete. India is witnessing an enormous upsurge in the number of youth taking up varied fields as their career. Considering the experience of the renowned personalities, their interview inspires and guides the youth very much. Based upon the convenience, it can be an email interview or a video call interview. In case of email interview, we send the questions to the concerned authority via email after which they can send the answers. In case of video interview, we use any of the interfaces upon mutual consideration. We also send a copy of our newsletter Tiresia after it is published. This section of Tiresia is very interesting and informative at the same time.

Vagmita

Vagmita is an open for all poetry recitation event, which involves astonishing and creative use of literary devices, all in a bundle of lines. The participant is supposed to e-mail us their entries through gmail. The video should comprise the student reciting any poem that is either self-composed or that of any renowned poet. The event brings out the hidden potential of many students who have the talent, but are hesitant to showcase it due to the lack of pertinent opportunities or camera consciousness. It focuses on literary, as well the oratory skills, expressions, dialogue-delivery, etc. qualities of a student.
